Cidercade Austin

Cidercade Austin is one of Austin’s most unique entertainment venues, combining an all-you-can-play arcade with a massive selection of house-made and craft ciders. It blends nostalgic gaming with a modern bar experience, making it a popular spot for casual nights out, group hangouts, and low-pressure socializing.

The concept is simple: one entry fee grants unlimited access to hundreds of arcade machines, from classic retro games to newer multiplayer titles. The result is a space that feels playful, energetic, and intentionally unpretentious.

What Makes It Stand Out

The appeal of Cidercade comes from its combination of unlimited gaming and a relaxed drink-focused environment. It removes the usual coin-op pressure of arcades and replaces it with a free-play model that encourages exploration and long stays.

  • Hundreds of free-play arcade and pinball machines
  • Unlimited play with single entry admission
  • Wide selection of house-made and craft ciders
  • Large open floor plan with industrial arcade feel
  • Strong social, group-friendly atmosphere

The Experience

A visit to Cidercade Austin feels like stepping into a full-scale arcade playground. Guests move freely between machines, competing in games, revisiting nostalgic favorites, or discovering new arcade setups.

“It’s part arcade, part bar, and part nostalgia trip that never really ends.”

The cider selection is a major part of the experience, offering a range of flavors from dry and crisp to fruity and sweet. It’s designed to be approachable even for people who don’t typically drink cider.

Atmosphere

The atmosphere at Cidercade Austin is lively, casual, and high-energy without feeling overwhelming. The large space helps distribute crowds, so even busy nights feel comfortable and open.

  1. Flat entry fee includes unlimited arcade play
  2. Best visited with groups for multiplayer games
  3. Can get busy on weekend evenings
  4. Large indoor space with industrial design
  5. Good mix of retro and modern arcade machines

Best Time to Visit

Weekday evenings offer a more relaxed experience with shorter waits for popular machines, while weekends bring higher energy and larger social groups. Early arrivals help maximize playtime before peak crowds.

Overall Impression

Cidercade Austin delivers one of the most fun and accessible entertainment experiences in Austin, combining nostalgia, social gaming, and casual drinks into a single, high-energy space.

Perfect for arcade lovers, group hangouts, casual drinks, and a relaxed but entertaining night out in Austin.
